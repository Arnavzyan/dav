Happy New Year - 2022.

I wish all my colleagues & friends at the DAV College Managing Committee and at the Arya Samaj, the very best in the coming year, 2022. May all have a peaceful, healthier and a fruitful year ahead

New Year is the time to make resolutions. Let us all resolve to create a better, healthier, and a peaceful environment. May all five elements of Nature be at peace with one another so that we can live without fear of this dreaded disease, COVID and all its variants.

Let us all resolve that we shall all work in greater cooperation to improve our DAV and create an environment conducive to build the future global citizens. I am certain that if each of us does his bit, DAV will soon become an organisation to be reckoned with and not only be the largest non-governmental educational organisation in India, but also be the best in the world.

Dear friends, the task before us today is to meet the challenges that COVID and Omicron are continuously throwing at us by forcing the educational system to change and evolve itself. Yet, we have to remain steadfast in our goal of man-making. I, on my behalf, assure you that I will work with all the energy to try to provide a more efficient , a more competitive, and a more robust DAV " साधन की कमी हो सकती हे, पर साधना में कमी नहीं आने देंगे ।”।

I pray to the Almighty to give us the health, strength and the wisdom to work towards our common goal to carry the flag of DAV to higher pedestals while the sound of AUM resonates from every heart.



Punam Suri
President
DAV College Managing Committee,
and Arya Pradeshik Pratinidhi Sabha


Managing Editor
Daily Milap, New Delhi
